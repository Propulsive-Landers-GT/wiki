# Installing Visio

### Follow Instructions to Install VISIO here:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (25).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Go to [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students)
2. Sign in/create an account with your **GT email**
3. On the search bar in the middle top of your screen once you're logged in, search for "dev tools"
4. Click on "Education" should be an icon with purple.
5. Now on the left beside "Learning resources" click "Software"
6. Within software, search up "Visio"
7. Install "Visio Professional 2021"

A video of this process can be found on our discord server under #Resources.

<mark style="color:red;">**Note:**</mark> **There is a web version, but that will not be enough!** All of our files require you to have the version that is installed on your laptop for you to view and edit. If you are propulsion or working on the feed system / engine in any way, this is a must.&#x20;
